Indigo Labs Defines Problems for Industries

Problem

Organizations create products and services at a breakneck speed by leveraging open source tech. However, they fail to take the time to ask "is this product helping our users in the long-term?". When teams lose sight of their users, they lose sight of their purpose. We like to start with figuring out the why. By defining the problem but starting with why, we pave the way for innovation that benefits the public. Our mission is to align design thinking with product development from the ground up.
